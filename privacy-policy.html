SportConnect Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use SportConnect.

1. Information We Collect

Profile Information: Name, email address, profile photos, sports interests, experience level, location, and bio.

Usage Data: How you interact with our app, messages sent, profiles viewed, and app performance data.

Device Information: Device type, operating system, IP address, and mobile device identifiers.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect location information to help you find nearby athletes and facilities.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our sports networking services
  • Connect you with other athletes, coaches, and sports facilities
  • Send you notifications about messages and matches
  • Ensure platform safety and prevent misuse
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

With Other Users: Your profile information is visible to other users to facilitate connections.

Service Providers: We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition, user data may be transferred as part of business assets.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure server infrastructure

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update: You can access and update your profile information at any time.

Delete Account: You can delete your account and all associated data through our app settings.

Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.

Opt-out: You can opt out of certain communications and data processing.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active.

Deleted Accounts: Most data is deleted immediately upon account deletion, with some data retained for legal compliance (up to 30 days).

Messages: Message history is deleted when either party deletes their account.

7. Children's Privacy

SportConnect is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@sportconnect.com
  • Support: support@sportconnect.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@sportconnect.com

11. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. You can:

  • Submit a data deletion request
  • Delete your account immediately
  • Contact our support team for assistance

This privacy policy is compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations. For specific regional privacy rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer.
